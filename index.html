Octavio Gutierrez

Software Engineer & Founder

Octavio is a software engineer with over 12 years of experience designing and building high-impact, scalable systems across e-commerce and enterprise software, most recently at Walmart Global Tech. With a strong foundation in backend architecture, cloud infrastructure, and end-to-end product development, he brings both technical depth and strategic thinking to every project. Currently based in the United States, Octavio is focused on innovation in health tech and enterprise SaaS, including the development of TheraLink, a digital mental health platform designed to empower individuals and underserved communities through accessible, secure, and developer-friendly tools.
